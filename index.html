Joint Entropy Search for Maximally-Informed Bayesian Optimization

Authors: Carl Hvarfner, Frank Hutter and Luigi Nardi

Conference: Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems (NeurIPS), 2022

In this paper, we propose Joint Entropy Search, a new acquisition function for Bayesian optimization that maximizes the mutual information between the minimum value found and the optimum of the unknown function being optimized.

Authors: Carl Hvarfner, Danny Stoll, Artur Souza, Marius Lindauer, Frank Hutter and Luigi Nardi

Conference: International Conference on Learning Representations (ICLR), 2022

In this paper, we propose a new Bayesian optimization method that incorporates user beliefs about the unknown function being optimized via a personalized acquisition function. We show that our approach leads to improved performance compared to standard Bayesian optimization methods.
